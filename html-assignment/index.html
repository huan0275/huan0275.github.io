About Me

Hello, my name is Caihua Huang. You can call me Henri. I come from China. I love traveling and cooking. My favorite sport is swimming, but currently I can't find a swimming pool here in Ottawa. Plus, it'd be very cold to swim outside in this season. I think it's possible to swim from Jun 21st, because that's the summer solstice. That should be warm. I traveled in Canada back in 2018 and it was summer. So everthing turned out to be very comfortable, unlike what the weather is now. I went to 3 provinces: BC, AB, and ON I love AB the most, because of the Lake Louise, which is gorgeous. But it's such a pity that I didn't get to visit Montreal due to the time limit. I guess when the winter ends, whenever that'll be, I'll spend some time visiting there and see how difficult to understand the Quebec French.
